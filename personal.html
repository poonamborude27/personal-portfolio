How it works

From submission to resolution, in four steps.

01

Submit

File details of your grievance through the form, with or without your name attached.

02

Acknowledged

You receive a tracking ID immediately and a confirmation within 24 hours.

03

Under review

The relevant committee investigates and may reach out for more information.

04

Resolved

You're notified of the outcome and any action taken, via your tracking ID.
